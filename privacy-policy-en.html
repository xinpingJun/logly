Privacy Policy

Logly Baby Tracker - Cherishing Every Moment of Growth

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Logly Baby Tracker! We understand how precious your baby's growth records are to your family, and we deeply respect your privacy concerns.

Important Statement: Logly is a purely local application. All data is stored entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or access any of your personal data or parenting records. We cannot view, modify, or delete any of your information.

By using Logly services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any terms of this policy, please discontinue using our services.

2. Data Storage Statement

Core Principle: Logly does not collect any data. All information below is stored only on your device locally and is completely under your control.

2.1 Locally Stored Data Types

The following data is generated by the app and saved only on your device:

  • Baby Profile: Name, date of birth, gender, profile photo
  • Feeding Records: Feeding time, type, amount, duration, etc.
  • Sleep Records: Sleep time, quality rating
  • Diaper Records: Change time, status, photos
  • Growth Records: Weight, height, head circumference data and photos
  • Vaccine Records: Vaccination schedule, status, notes

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Device model or operating system version
  • App usage frequency or statistics
  • Crash logs or diagnostic information
  • IP address or location information
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Features: Record and display feeding, sleep, diaper, growth, and vaccination data
  • Smart Statistics: Generate weekly/monthly/yearly statistics and visual charts
  • Growth Analysis: Compare with WHO growth standards and calculate percentiles
  • Reminder Services: Send feeding, diaper change, sleep, and vaccination reminders
  • Data Sync: Sync data across your devices via iCloud
  • Personalization: Customize themes and interface based on your preferences
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage data to optimize features and fix issues

4. Data Storage and Sync

Local-First Principle: All your parenting records are stored locally on your device by default. We prioritize your data privacy and security.

4.1 iCloud Sync

  • When you enable iCloud sync, data is synchronized across your devices through Apple's iCloud service
  • iCloud data storage follows Apple's privacy policy
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in Settings

4.2 Data Export

  • We provide data export functionality so you can back up records to files
  • Exported data files are managed by you

5. Photos and Media Files

The app may involve the following photo usage scenarios:

  • Baby Profile Photo: For personalizing the baby profile display
  • Growth Record Photos: To capture precious growth moments
  • Diaper Record Photos: For health observation (optional)
Important Notice: All photos are stored only on your device or in your iCloud account. We do not upload or access these photos.

6. Data Control and Deletion

You Have Complete Control: Since all data is stored only on your device, we cannot access, modify, or delete any of your information. You can manage your data through the following methods:
  • View Data: View all records anytime within the app
  • Edit Data: Modify or correct any record
  • Delete Data: Delete individual records or clear all data
  • Export Data: Export data to local files for backup
  • Complete Deletion: Uninstall the app to delete all local data

Important Note: Since we cannot access your data, you do not need to contact us to delete data. Please manage your data directly within the app or through your device settings.

7. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Use Apple's SwiftData framework for local encrypted data storage
  • iCloud sync uses Apple's end-to-end encryption technology
  • App supports Face ID/Touch ID lock to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular security audits and code reviews

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: View all recorded data at any time
  • Right to Edit: Modify or correct any record
  • Right to Delete: Delete individual records or clear all data
  • Right to Export: Export data to local files
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Stop using and delete the app at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Logly Baby Tracker is designed for parents to record their baby's growth. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All data is entered and managed by parents or legal guardians.

If you believe we have collected information from a child without parental consent, please contact us immediately for deletion.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised policy will be posted in the app and on our website, with the latest update date noted at the top.

For significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud: For data synchronization and backup, following Apple's privacy policy
  • WHO Growth Standards: For growth curve comparison, using only publicly available reference data

We do not integrate advertising SDKs, social sharing SDKs, or other third-party services that may collect user data.
